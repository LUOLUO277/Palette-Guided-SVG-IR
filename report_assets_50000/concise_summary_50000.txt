Under the PSNR_PBR-matched low-resolution protocol, Palette-Guided V1 improves albedo PSNR from 17.4766 to 19.8996 (+2.4230 dB). The tradeoff is PSNR_PBR dropping from 42.3922 to 38.6216 (-3.7706) and NVS PSNR dropping from 26.6381 to 25.9693 (-0.6688). This interpretation remains a decomposition-vs-fitting tradeoff: palette regularization improves albedo consistency at the evaluation resolution used by rendering, while render-fitting metrics still decline.
